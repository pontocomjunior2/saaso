**New models (add after existing models):**

1. `StageRule` — one rule per stage. Fields: `id` (uuid), `stageId` (String, @unique), `tenantId` (String), `isActive` (Boolean @default(true)), relations to `stage` (Stage), `tenant` (Tenant), `steps` (StageRuleStep[]), `runs` (StageRuleRun[]), timestamps. Index: `@@index([tenantId, stageId])`.

2. `StageRuleStep` — ordered steps in a rule. Fields: `id`, `ruleId`, `order` (Int), `dayOffset` (Int — 0=D0, 1=D+1, etc.), `channel` (CampaignChannel), `messageTemplateId` (FK to StageMessageTemplate), timestamps. Constraints: `@@unique([ruleId, order])`, `@@index([ruleId, order])`.

3. `StageRuleRun` — active execution per card. Fields: `id`, `ruleId`, `cardId`, `tenantId`, `status` (StageRuleRunStatus @default(PENDING)), `triggerSource` (String — "CARD_ENTERED" or "MANUAL"), `startedAt`, `completedAt`, `pausedAt`, `canceledAt` (all DateTime?), `lastError` (String?), relations, timestamps. Indexes: `@@index([tenantId, status])`, `@@index([cardId, status])`.

4. `StageRuleRunStep` — individual step execution. Fields: `id`, `runId`, `tenantId` (String), `ruleStepId` (FK to StageRuleStep), `order` (Int), `channel` (CampaignChannel), `scheduledFor` (DateTime), `status` (StageRuleRunStepStatus @default(PENDING)), `startedAt`, `completedAt` (DateTime?), `attempts` (Int @default(0)), `lastError` (String?), `externalMessageId` (String?), timestamps. Constraints: `@@unique([runId, order])`, `@@index([tenantId, status, scheduledFor])`.

5. `MetaWebhookMapping` — form-to-stage mapping. Fields: `id`, `tenantId`, `metaFormId` (String @unique), `pipelineId`, `stageId`, `verifyToken` (String), `pageAccessToken` (String?), timestamps. Index: `@@index([tenantId])`. Relations to Tenant, Pipeline, Stage.

6. `MetaLeadIngestion` — dedup log. Fields: `id`, `tenantId` (String), `metaLeadId` (String @unique), `cardId` (String?), relation to Card (onDelete: SetNull), `createdAt`.
